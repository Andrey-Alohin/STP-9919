Relax, Think, Untangle — One Knot at a Time

Play & Untangle Now

Game Description

Welcome to Tangled Rope: Twisted Puzzle, a deceptively simple game that will challenge your mind and soothe your senses.Your mission? Untangle ropes, one knot at a time.

At first, it may seem easy. But as the ropes multiply and the puzzles become more twisted, only your logic and focus will guide you forward. This game is designed for thinkers, meditators, and casual puzzlers alike — offering a rewarding sense of clarity with every level cleared.

Key Features

  • Progressive Challenge:

    Levels grow in difficulty, unlocking advanced mechanics like looping ropes, fixed nodes, and hidden overlaps.

  • Addictive Puzzle Logic:

    Unravel complex knots by dragging, lifting, and sliding ropes into place.

  • Clean, Colorful Design:

    Vibrant rope visuals, minimalist UI, and relaxing animations for an elegant play experience.

  • Soothing Gameplay:

    Designed to help you relax while stimulating your mind. Perfect for breaks, bedtime, or quiet mornings.

  • Hints & Solutions:

    Use smart hints or reveal solutions when you’re stuck — no frustration, just learning.

  • Simple Controls:

    Tap, drag, untangle. That’s all you need to know.

  • Brain Progression Tracker:

    Watch your stats evolve as you master levels and train your spatial reasoning.

Game Modes

  • Classic Untangle: Solve one tangled mess at a time
  • Timed Challenge: Beat the rope puzzle before the clock runs out
  • Zen Mode:No timers, just smooth puzzles and calm music
  • Daily Puzzle: New rope configuration each day
  • Puzzle Builder (Coming Soon): Design your own tangles and share with friends
